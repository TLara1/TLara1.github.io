Other Interests

Rock Climbing

I have climbed since sixth grade, focusing mostly on bouldering. Some of my notable ascents include:

  • God Module V11, Horspens 40, AL
  • Mega Man V11, Hospital Boulders, AL
  • Pocket Sands V10/11, Hospital Boulders, AL
  • Golden Harvest V10, Rocktown, GA

I also occasionally partake in local climbing competitions. Over the last two years, I have ended as a finalist in these competitions:

  • Rockspot Brookline Spooky Send-Off 2024
  • Benchmark Climbing Battle of the Beast 2024
  • Central Rock Gym Harvard Ritual 2024
  • Central Rock Gym Framingham Flaming Ham 2023

Clarinet Playing

Playing at a concert with the MIT Ribotones.
I have played the clarinet for 12 years, first with the Decatur High School Wind Ensemble and currently with the MIT Wind Ensemble. In high school I played with the Georgia District Honour band and more recently, I played for two years with the MIT Video Game Orchestra (VGO). I also occasionally play with chamber groups such as the MIT Ribotones .
My favourite pieces include Crussel's Third Clarinet Concerto Op. 11, Busoni's Clarient Concertino Op. 47, Cavallini's Adagio e Tarantella, and Weber's Clarinet Concertino Op. 26.

Origami

Board, War, and Role-Playing Games

My beloved 0-5 Seraphon.

In the little free time I do have, I enjoy playing games, particularly with the MIT Strategic Game Society (SGS). My favourite board games include Spirit Island, Oath, Caverna, Barrage, War of the Ring, and of course, Twilight Imperium. I also played a good amount of Warhammer: Age of Sigmar back in high school, although I am less able to indulge my miniature-painting desires nowadays. I have also recently begun exploring role-playing games, hosting a Dungeon World campaign for my friends over the last month.
